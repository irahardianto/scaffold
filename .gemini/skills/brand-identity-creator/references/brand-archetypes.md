# Brand Archetypes

Brand archetypes are 12 character personas that help define the "soul" of a brand. They are based on Jungian psychology and help build a connection with the target audience.

## 1. The Innocent
*   **Goal**: To be happy.
*   **Traits**: Optimistic, honest, humble.
*   **Strategy**: Do things right.
*   **Examples**: Dove, Coca-Cola.

## 2. The Everyman (The Regular Guy/Gal)
*   **Goal**: To belong.
*   **Traits**: Down-to-earth, supportive, relatable.
*   **Strategy**: Develop ordinary solid virtues, be real.
*   **Examples**: IKEA, Home Depot.

## 3. The Hero
*   **Goal**: To exert mastery in a way that improves the world.
*   **Traits**: Courageous, bold, inspirational.
*   **Strategy**: Become as strong and competent as possible.
*   **Examples**: Nike, FedEx.

## 4. The Outlaw (The Rebel)
*   **Goal**: To overturn what isn't working.
*   **Traits**: Rebellious, wild, paving the way.
*   **Strategy**: Disrupt, destroy, or shock.
*   **Examples**: Harley-Davidson, Virgin.

## 5. The Explorer
*   **Goal**: To find out who you are through exploring the world.
*   **Traits**: Independent, ambitious, spiritual.
*   **Strategy**: Journey, seeking out and experiencing new things.
*   **Examples**: Jeep, The North Face, Patagonia.

## 6. The Creator
*   **Goal**: To realize a vision.
*   **Traits**: Creative, imaginative, artistic.
*   **Strategy**: Develop artistic control and skill.
*   **Examples**: Apple, Lego, Adobe.

## 7. The Ruler
*   **Goal**: To create a prosperous, successful family or community.
*   **Traits**: Leader, responsible, organized.
*   **Strategy**: Exercise power.
*   **Examples**: Mercedes-Benz, Rolex.

## 8. The Magician
*   **Goal**: To make dreams come true.
*   **Traits**: Visionary, charismatic, imaginative.
*   **Strategy**: Develop a vision and live by it.
*   **Examples**: Disney, Dyson.

## 9. The Lover
*   **Goal**: To be in a relationship with the people, work, and surroundings they love.
*   **Traits**: Passionate, intimate, soothing.
*   **Strategy**: Become more and more physically and emotionally attractive.
*   **Examples**: Chanel, Victoria's Secret.

## 10. The Caregiver
*   **Goal**: To help others.
*   **Traits**: Compassionate, generous, parent-like.
*   **Strategy**: Do things for others.
*   **Examples**: Johnson & Johnson, WWF.

## 11. The Jester
*   **Goal**: To have a great time and lighten up the world.
*   **Traits**: Fun, light-hearted, mischievous.
*   **Strategy**: Play, make jokes, be funny.
*   **Examples**: Old Spice, M&M's.

## 12. The Sage
*   **Goal**: To use intelligence and analysis to understand the world.
*   **Traits**: Wisdom, intelligence, truth-seeking.
*   **Strategy**: Seek out information and knowledge.
*   **Examples**: Google, BBC, TED.
